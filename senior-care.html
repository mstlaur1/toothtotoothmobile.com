Senior Care

Compassionate dental care tailored for seniors and people with dementia

Specialized Care for Seniors

Tooth to Tooth is proud to offer tailored care for seniors and people with dementia. We understand the unique challenges these patients face, which is why we are committed to providing them with the time they need for frequent breaks, creating a calm and soothing environment.

Our team demonstrates patience and understanding, ensuring that each senior feels respected and cared for during their care, to enhance their dental experience and overall well-being.

Experience You Can Trust

Specialized Training

Joy has been taking care of those with reduced mobility and cognitive impairments for years. She is well experienced to handle the unique needs of senior dental care.

Patient-Centered Approach

We provide the time needed for frequent breaks and create a calm, soothing environment. Each senior is treated with respect and dignity throughout their care.

Mobile Service Available

For seniors with mobility challenges, our mobile service brings professional dental care directly to your home or care facility.
