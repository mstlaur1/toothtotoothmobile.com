Our Services

Dental Cleaning

Professional scaling and cleaning prevent gum disease, cavities, and bad breath. Regular scaling, cleaning, and dental care are essential for preventing gum disease, cavities, and bad breath.

Polishing & Stain Removal

Removal of unsightly stains and polishing the teeth is an essential part of your dental hygiene, removing build up and bacteria and gives you that 'fresh from the dental clinic feeling.'

Oral Hygiene Instructions

Provided during appointments in residences, offices, or homes. Includes proper brushing and flossing techniques, and guidance on dental products to help you maintain optimal oral health.

Fluoride Application

Available for adults and children to strengthen enamel and reduce cavity risk. Fluoride also helps alleviate tooth sensitivity, providing comfort and protection.

Mobile Service

Care at Your Doorstep

We bring professional dental hygiene care directly to your home or office with full infection control compliance. Our mobile service covers Montreal, West Island, Vaudreuil, Rigaud, Hudson and surrounding areas, following all dental regulations and infection control protocols.

About Joy Maderazo, RDH

Joy graduated in dentistry in the Philippines before immigrating to Canada in 1990. She graduated from John Abbott College in Montreal with dental hygiene credentials in 2010 and worked as a hygienist for 10 years before establishing her independent practice.

Joy emphasizes personalized care and staying current with dental trends. She specializes in senior care, including patients with dementia and reduced mobility. Her gentle approach ensures comfort and dignity for all patients.

Joy is committed to providing the highest quality of care with a personalized approach to each patient, ensuring everyone feels comfortable and well-cared for.
