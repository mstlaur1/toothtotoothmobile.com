Mobile Service

We bring professional dental care directly to your home or office with full infection control compliance.

Requirements for Mobile Service

  • Suitable parking for a full-size SUV
  • Adequate ventilation (window or air system)
  • Available electrical outlet
  • Bathroom and sink access

Senior Care Specialization

We have extensive experience providing dental care for seniors, including those with dementia and reduced mobility. Our gentle approach ensures comfort and dignity for all patients.

Our mobile service is particularly beneficial for seniors who have difficulty traveling to dental appointments.

About Joy Maderazo, RDH

Joy graduated in dentistry in the Philippines and relocated to Canada in 1990. She completed her dental hygiene training at John Abbott College in 2010 and has been providing personalized care in private practice for over 10 years.

Joy is a member of the Ordre des hygiénistes dentaires du Québec and is committed to providing the highest quality of care with a personalized approach to each patient.
